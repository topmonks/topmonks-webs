What Can We Do for You?

Blockchain development

We will provide the entire blockchain technology service; from core blockchain development to decentralized applications on the top of it. We can connect the developed blockchain to a particular existing API of the centralized application.

dApps development

We trained our developers to build a bulletproof and error-free smartcontracts. They can prepare the whole set of smartcontracts for your ICO, and also develop your mobile/web dApp's interface. The dTeam provide a full package service; UX design, dApp back-end, dApp front-end, smartcontracts.

ICO

You can make use of our graphic designers who will prepare the whole visual identity; from landing page to dApp UX design. We are ready to kick off ICO, KYC or AML for you.

Infrastructure

You shall not underestimate the hosting of infrastructure and its security. We know what is needed to be done and we will set it up for you. Decentralizovane projekty vyzaduju decentralizovanu architekturu, vieme vam pomoct so setupom your nodes and master nodes, make it always scalable and manage its security.

Consultations

If not sure, where to start with your ICO or dApp, you can discuss your current idea with us. We can help you with matters of Security audits, cryptography, architecture, training, testing, code review, code update, and everything else that is part of our skillset.

Security matters and you should not take it lightly. Any blockchain network is only as secure as its infrastructure.

Security is a crucial part of the development process that we focus on meticulously because once you put your smartcontracts or anything else live, into the blockchain, you can't take it back.

We can check your security and provide feedback with the particular steps you can take to make your product more secure. Let's keep your transaction data safe.
